What does LPG have to offer?

LPG consists of approximately 25 physicians, lab and x-ray technicians, nurses and assistants, as well as front office staff. In the community in which LPG operates, they serve approximately 3,000 patients, delivering services such as emergency medical treatment, wellness checkups, Oncology, Chiropractic, Cardiology, well as many other medical offerings
